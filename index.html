OUR MOST WANTED

Our saddles stand as a testament to unparalleled craftsmanship and unwavering dedication to excellence. Crafted with precision and passion, each saddle embodies the highest standards of quality, ensuring superior performance and comfort for both horse and rider.

Loire

This french styled close contact saddle is built for daily riding and competition. It showcases a semi-deep seat with a narrow twist designed for jumping and comfortable riding. This seat is built over a wood laminated spring tree reinforced with steel. The Loire is available with both wool flocked panels, as well as injected foam panels.

Arthur

A balanced and comfortable saddle, the Arthur is built to meet the needs of all dressage ridings. The saddle showcases a narrow-twisted seat built over a wood laminated spring tree. Outfitted with wool flocked panels or foam panels specially designed to prevent lumps. It is also equipped with long billet straps with a V system to keep the saddle in a perfect position.

More Info
Bayon

A traditional close contact saddle. Perfect for jumping, eventing, and/or cross country. It features a semi-deep seat with a narrow twist built over an injected plastic tree. Its flaps are soft-covered with an extra layer of leather that gives a better grip to the rider and provides overall extra comfort.

Alissa

A saddle designed to meet the demands of a serious dressage rider with a keen sense of style. The saddle showcases a narrow-twisted seat built over a wood laminated spring tree. Outfitted with wool flocked panels or a thin placed over a thin layer of foam against the horse to prevent lumps. This saddle model provides a long block with a straighter leg that gives a more secure riding position.
